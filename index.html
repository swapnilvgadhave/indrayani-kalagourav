About · माहिती

Indrayani Kalagourav Foundation preserves local arts and provides education through scholarships, workshops, and community programs.
इंद्रायणी कलागौरव फाउंडेशन स्थानिक कला जतन व शैक्षणिक उपक्रमांद्वारे सामाजिक विकासासाठी कार्य करते.

Programs · कार्यक्रम

  • Education Support · शैक्षणिक सहयोग: Scholarships, mentoring, coaching, book distribution, digital learning.
  • Cultural Workshops · सांस्कृतिक कार्यशाळा: Music, dance, art classes, artist residencies, and youth showcases.
  • Community · समुदाय: Health camps, environmental drives, and volunteer programs.
