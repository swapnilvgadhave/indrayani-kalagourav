About · माहिती

Indrayani Kalagourav Foundation preserves local arts and provides education through scholarships, workshops and community programs.
इंद्रायणी कलागौरव फाउंडेशन स्थानिक कला जतन करते व शिष्यवृत्ती, कार्यशाळा व समाजोपयोगी कार्यक्रमाद्वारे शिक्षण देते.

Programs · कार्यक्रम
  • Education Support · शैक्षणिक सहाय्य: Scholarships, mentoring, study materials, coaching camps, book distribution, digital learning support.
  • Cultural Workshops · सांस्कृतिक कार्यशाळा: Music, dance, craft classes, artist residencies and youth showcases.
  • Community · समुदाय: Health camps, environmental drives, village skill training and volunteer programs.

Preserve culture. Educate the next generation.
