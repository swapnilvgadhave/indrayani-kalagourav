About us

Indrayani Kalagourav Foundation is a non-profit organization founded to promote education, preserve cultural heritage, and support rural community development in the Indrayani river basin of Maharashtra.

Key facts

Established
2024
Registered as

Mission
To empower youth through education and sustain regional cultural identity through awareness, art, and community work.

Programs

Education Support

Scholarships, after-school tuition, and mentorship for rural students.

Cultural Workshops

Music, dance, art, and traditional craft learning sessions for youth.

Community Development

Local clean-up drives, social events, and awareness programs in the Indrayani region.
